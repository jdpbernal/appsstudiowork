Flashlight App Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 16, 2025

Welcome to the Flashlight App by Apps Studio Work. This Privacy Policy is specific to our Flashlight application and explains our approach to privacy, emphasizing that we do not collect, use, or disclose any personal information from any of our users.

About Our Flashlight App

Our Flashlight App is a simple utility application that provides flashlight functionality using your device's built-in camera flash. The app is designed to be straightforward and user-friendly, with no unnecessary features that could compromise your privacy.

Our No-Data Collection Commitment

We are committed to protecting your privacy. Our Flashlight App:

  • Does not collect any personal information
  • Does not track your location
  • Does not access your contacts, photos, or other personal data
  • Does not use analytics tools to track your usage
  • Does not contain any advertisements
  • Does not require any user accounts

Device Permissions

Our Flashlight App requires only the minimum permissions necessary to function:

  • Camera flash access: This permission is essential for the app to control your device's flashlight

We do not request any other permissions that are not directly related to the core functionality of the app.

Children's Privacy

While our Flashlight App is generally intended for adults, we comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA). We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children under 13 years of age. Since our app does not collect any data at all, children's privacy is inherently protected.

Third-Party Services

Our Flashlight App does not integrate with any third-party services, analytics tools, or advertising networks. This means there is no sharing of information with external entities because no information is collected in the first place.

Security

Even though we do not collect any personal information, we are committed to ensuring the security of our application. We regularly update our app to address any potential security vulnerabilities.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page, and we encourage users to review our Privacy Policy periodically.

Privacy Regulation Compliance

Our no-data collection approach ensures compliance with various privacy regulations worldwide, including GDPR, CCPA, CPRA, VCDPA, and PIPEDA. Since we do not collect, process, or store any personal data, we naturally comply with these regulations' requirements.
