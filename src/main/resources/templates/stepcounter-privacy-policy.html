Step Counter App Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 16, 2025

Welcome to the Step Counter App by Apps Studio Work. This Privacy Policy is specific to our Step Counter application and explains our approach to privacy, including what information we collect, how we use it, and the steps we take to protect your data.

About Our Step Counter App

Our Step Counter App is a fitness utility application that helps users track their daily steps, distance walked, and calories burned. The app is designed to be user-friendly and privacy-focused, collecting only the minimum data necessary to provide its core functionality.

Information We Collect

To provide our step tracking service, we collect the following information:

  • Step count data from your device's sensors
  • Basic fitness information you choose to provide (height, weight, age) for calorie calculations
  • App usage statistics to improve our service

All data is stored locally on your device by default. You can optionally enable cloud backup of your data, which will require creating an account.

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information solely for the following purposes:

  • To track and display your step count and related fitness metrics
  • To calculate calories burned based on your activity and provided information
  • To improve the app's functionality and user experience
  • To provide cloud backup services if you opt in

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

Device Permissions

Our Step Counter App requires the following permissions to function:

  • Motion & fitness sensors: To count steps and track movement
  • Storage access: To save your fitness data and preferences
  • Internet access: For cloud backup (optional) and app updates

We do not request any other permissions that are not directly related to the core functionality of the app.

Data Security

We take the security of your data seriously. All data transmitted to our servers (if you opt for cloud backup) is encrypted using industry-standard protocols. We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

Children's Privacy

Our Step Counter App is not directed to children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we learn that we have collected personal information from a child under 13, we will promptly delete that information.

Third-Party Services

Our app may integrate with third-party fitness platforms (such as Google Fit or Apple Health) if you choose to connect them. When you enable these integrations, we only share the minimum data necessary for the integration to function. Please review the privacy policies of these third-party services for information on how they handle your data.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal information at any time. You can do this directly through the app settings or by contacting us using the information provided below.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page, and we encourage users to review our Privacy Policy periodically.

Privacy Regulation Compliance

Our app is designed to comply with various privacy regulations worldwide, including GDPR, CCPA, CPRA, VCDPA, and PIPEDA. We collect only the minimum data necessary to provide our service and give you control over your information.
