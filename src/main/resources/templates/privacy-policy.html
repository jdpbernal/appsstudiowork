Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 30, 2025

Welcome to Apps Studio Work. Our applications and services are generally intended for adults and designed primarily with adults in mind. We are committed to protecting the privacy of all our users by implementing a strict no-data-collection policy. This Privacy Policy explains our approach to privacy, emphasizing that we do not collect, use, or disclose any personal information from any of our users.

Definition of Services

Throughout this privacy policy, "Services" refers to our Games, Websites and related services and other products we control.

Children's Privacy

While our services are generally intended for adults, we comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA). Although our applications and services are designed primarily with adults in mind, we are committed to protecting the privacy of all users and do not collect any personal information from any users, regardless of age.

Our No-Data Approach

We do not collect any personal information from any users, regardless of age. Our applications and services are designed to operate without collecting or storing any user data.

Parental Guidance

While our services are primarily designed for adults, we recognize that younger users may occasionally access our services. We encourage parents to be involved in their children's online activities. If parents have any questions or concerns about their child's use of our applications or services, they can contact us at jdpbernal@appsstudiowork.com.

Our No-Data Policy

We do not collect, use, or share any personal information from our users. Our applications and services are designed to function without the need to collect, store, or process any user data.

This means:

  • We do not track user activity
  • We do not use cookies or similar tracking technologies
  • We do not share any information with third parties
  • We do not have any user accounts that store personal information

Security Commitment

Even though we do not collect any personal information, we are committed to maintaining the security of our applications and services to ensure a safe experience for all users.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page, and we encourage users to review our Privacy Policy periodically.

No Data Collection Notice

Unlike many websites and applications, we do not collect any user information. This includes:

  • No collection of browser information
  • No tracking of operating system
  • No recording of IP addresses
  • No monitoring of pages visited or time spent
  • No tracking of referring websites

Our applications and services are designed to function without the need for any user data collection.
