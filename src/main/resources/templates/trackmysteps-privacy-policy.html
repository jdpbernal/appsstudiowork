Track My Steps App Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 16, 2025

Welcome to the Track My Steps App by Apps Studio Work. This Privacy Policy is specific to our step tracking application and explains our approach to privacy, emphasizing that we do not collect, use, or disclose any personal information from any of our users.

About Our Track My Steps App

Our Track My Steps App is a fitness utility that helps you monitor your daily physical activity by counting your steps, tracking distance walked, and estimating calories burned. The app uses your device's built-in sensors to provide these features without requiring an account or online connectivity.

Our No-Data Collection Commitment

We are committed to protecting your privacy. Our Track My Steps App:

  • Does not collect any personal information
  • Does not track your location
  • Does not access your contacts, photos, or other personal data
  • Does not use analytics tools to track your usage
  • Does not contain any advertisements
  • Does not require any user accounts

Device Permissions

Our Track My Steps App requires only the minimum permissions necessary to function:

  • Motion & fitness sensors: Essential for counting steps and tracking movement
  • Physical activity recognition: Required to distinguish between different types of movement

We do not request any other permissions that are not directly related to the core functionality of the app.

Children's Privacy

Our Track My Steps App is designed for users of all ages, including children. We comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA). Since our app does not collect or transmit any personal information, children's privacy is inherently protected when using our app.

Third-Party Services

Our Track My Steps App does not integrate with any third-party services, analytics tools, or advertising networks. This means there is no sharing of information with external entities.

Security

Even though we do not collect or transmit personal information, we are committed to ensuring the security of our application. We regularly update our app to address any potential security vulnerabilities and to improve performance.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page, and we encourage users to review our Privacy Policy periodically.

Privacy Regulation Compliance

Our approach to data handling ensures compliance with various privacy regulations worldwide, including GDPR, CCPA, CPRA, VCDPA, and PIPEDA. Since we do not collect, process, or store any personal data on our servers, we naturally comply with these regulations' requirements.
